# Business Assumptions

## Assumption 1

Exited = 1 is treated as a proxy for a deposit runoff event.

Rationale:
The public dataset does not contain actual deposit withdrawal records. Customer attrition is therefore used as a reasonable proxy for customer runoff behaviour.

## Assumption 2

Customer balance represents the potential exposure at risk.

Expected Deposit at Risk = Balance × Predicted Runoff Probability

## Assumption 3

Model outputs are intended for early warning purposes rather than exact cashflow forecasting.
